Site Name:

ServiceHub Chamber

Site Purpose:

This website is intended to help local service providers gain better exposure in 
Bogotá by offering a platform that connects clients with trusted professionals. 
It will provide a wide range of essential services such as plumbing, welding, 
carpentry, painting, and many other everyday home and maintenance services. Each 
contractor or service provider will be reviewed and will have a catalog showcasing 
their work, making their profiles more attractive to potential clients and helping 
them reach a wider audience.

Target Market

Services providers and citicenz in the city of Bogota.

Site Goals:

• Provide high-quality and qualified services to clients
• Create a bridge between customer needs and professional solutions
• Improve the visibility of skilled professionals and contractors
• Promote a culture of quality work, trust, and professionalism

Users:

• Emergency Locksmith Client:
Sofia is a 29-year-old professional who accidentally lost her apartment keys late 
at night. She needs a reliable locksmith service that can respond quickly, provide 
secure solutions, and offer verified reviews to ensure safety and trust.

• Homeowner Looking for Painting Services:
Carlos is a 42-year-old homeowner preparing to renovate his house. He is searching 
for experienced painters with portfolios of previous work, fair pricing, and positive 
customer feedback to help him choose the right professional.

• Family Needing Moving Services:
Andrea and her family are relocating to a new apartment across the city. They are 
looking for trustworthy moving services that can safely transport furniture and 
personal belongings while offering affordable prices and flexible schedules.

• Student Searching for an English Teacher:
Mateo is a 21-year-old university student who wants to improve his English skills 
for better job opportunities. He is looking for qualified English teachers with good
reviews, flexible schedules, and online or in-person class options.

Scenarios:

• A plumber wants to expand his customer base and gain more visibility in Bogotá. He 
visits the website to create a professional profile, upload photos of completed 
plumbing projects, and receive customer reviews that help attract new clients.

• A carpenter is looking for more job opportunities and wants to showcase custom 
furniture and woodworking projects. She uses the website to publish her services, 
display a portfolio of her work, and connect with homeowners interested in 
renovations and custom designs.

• An electrician wants to promote his repair and installation services to people in 
the community. He joins the platform to share his experience, provide contact 
information, and allow customers to leave ratings and feedback about his work.

• A private math tutor wants to reach more students who need academic support. She 
creates a profile on the website to display her qualifications, teaching experience, 
available schedules, and testimonials from previous students.

SEO Plan:

• Use relevant keywords such as “plumber in Bogotá,” “carpenter services,” 
“electrician near me,” and “home services in Bogotá” throughout the website content 
and headings.

• Optimize page titles, meta descriptions, and image alt text to improve visibility 
in search engine results.

• Create service-specific pages with clear descriptions, customer reviews, and 
location-based content to increase local SEO performance.

• Improve website performance with mobile-friendly design, fast loading speeds, and 
clean navigation to enhance user experience and search rankings.
